Welcome To AMS

AMS, the First International School in Benha, offers a broad and balanced educational approach supported by a wide range of curricular activities which equip all students with the skills they need in all subject areas. AMS uses The American Educational System, which are based on high standards (CCSS) of achievements. As a result, AMS students are widely accepted in all Egyptian Universities, as well as, most universities around the world. AMS is authorized by Cogina, which is the largest education improvement organization in the world.

AMS: The Academic Life

At AMS, your childern’s experience in these years will orbit around building essential skills in pre-literacy, curiosity, discovery, and cooperation. We aim for 3 main goals: making learning relevant and meaningful to students by adopting inquiry-based, integrated curriculum which focuses on the child as a learner, teaching academic skills, but also the interpersonal skills of team work, personal responsibility, and training them to understand, analyze, and evaluate ideas, arguments, findings, and the entire picture .Our final target is to build independent thinkers.

AMS: Our Courses

AMS provides perfect improvments in the students academic level throughout high-class courses. Our courses are organized and taught in the American Diploma style, which is effective and exquisite, leading our students in the right way to have an outstanding bank of knowledege. Some of the courses we allocate are Porgramming, SAT, Economics, and others such as ELA, Biology, and Math.
