Welcome To AMS

AMS, the First International School in Benha, offers a broad and balanced educational approach supported by a wide range of curricular activities which equip all students with the skills they need in all subject areas. AMS uses The American Educational System, which are based on high standards (CCSS) of achievements. As a result, AMS students are widely accepted in all Egyptian Universities, as well as, most universities around the world. AMS is authorized by Cogina, which is the largest education improvement organization in the world.
